Did you ever play the game ‘Telephone?’

Where you sat in a circle and whispered a sentence to each other, and by the end of the circle the words changed drastically—hilariously?

Telephone pictionary is similar, but it alternates between writing a sentence about a picture and drawing a picture of a sentence. The results are wild, wacky, and often oscene.

This is an archive showing how five crazy sentences can become five even crazier sentences, one piece of paper at a time.
